About me

I am a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU), India, specializing in Cyber Security and Digital Forensics of Robotics, Cyber-Physical Systems, and Emerging Technologies. My research investigates the security and forensics of robotic and embedded environments; vulnerability analysis, forensic frameworks, and threats facing modern intelligent systems.

My contributions span 25+ publications, 3 patents, 10+ invited talks, and multiple research grants including flagship work on ART-ADFIS-R, RoArch (RoboShell + RuFo), RoboXploit, and RoboAURA for securing and investigating robotic systems.

Research focus

  • Robotic Security

    Securing robotic and embedded systems against emerging attacks.

  • Robotic Forensics

    Investigating and reconstructing incidents in robotic environments.

  • Cyber-Physical Security & Forensics

    Protecting and analyzing connected physical-digital systems.

  • Emerging Technologies Security & Forensics

    Threat and forensic analysis for next-generation intelligent systems.
